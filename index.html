About

Kristen Ellis is a Board Certified Behavior Analyst (BCBA) with professional experience supporting children and families affected by autism and developmental challenges.

She earned her education from Johns Hopkins University and James Madison University and provides compassionate, evidence-based behavioral services designed to help families thrive.

Her work focuses on individualized support, family collaboration, and practical strategies that make meaningful improvements in everyday life.
