About

Kristen Ellis is a Board Certified Behavior Analyst (BCBA) with over six years of professional experience supporting children and families affected by autism and developmental challenges.

She earned her education from Johns Hopkins University and James Madison University and provides personalized, compassionate, evidence-based behavioral services designed to help families thrive.

Services are focused on individualized support, family collaboration, and practical strategies that make a real difference in daily life.
