About

Kristen Ellis is a Board Certified Behavior Analyst (BCBA) with over six years of professional experience supporting children and families affected by autism and developmental challenges.

She earned her education from Johns Hopkins University and James Madison University and provides compassionate, evidence-based behavioral services designed to help families thrive.

Her work focuses on individualized support, collaboration with families, and practical strategies that create meaningful progress in daily life.
